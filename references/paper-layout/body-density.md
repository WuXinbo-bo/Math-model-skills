# Body density contract

Effective body units count Chinese characters plus English words after excluding LaTeX commands, formulas, code blocks, figures, tables, comments, and Markdown markup.

- CUMCM and 51MCM default minimum: 3,500 effective units.
- MCM/ICM default minimum: 3,000 English-word-equivalent units.
- Each detected per-question chapter must have at least 450 effective units.
- Each per-question chapter must contain model/mechanism content and result content.
- The manuscript as a whole must contain validation/sensitivity evidence and result interpretation.
- These are floors against hollow papers, not targets for padding. Passing the numeric floor does not excuse a missing scientific link.
